About us (The Developer)
I am a solo developer who has created this app to help people with their mental health.
I have a passion for creating apps that make a difference in people's lives.
I believe that mental health is just as important as physical health, and I want to help people take care of their mental health.
I have created this app to be a safe space for people to reflect on their thoughts and feelings, and to help them grow and heal.
I hope that this app can help you on your mental health journey.
Thank you for using my app.
